Besan Pancake

Description

A healthy and very nutrious pancake mafe from chick pea flour.

Ingredients

  • 50g Besan
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2tsp salt
  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Add eggs and a dash of water and whisk to combine
  3. Cook in a frying pan over medium heat and set and golden on both sides
